Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how SpeachyAI ("we", "our", "us", "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects user ("you", "your") data while using our services. By using SpeachyAI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from users:

  • Email address (for registration and verification)
  • Name and username
  • Google or Apple account information (for sign-in)
  • AI characters created by the user
  • Messages exchanged within the app
  • Favorited bots and users
  • Profile avatar
  • Preferred app language
  • Subscription plan (Free, Premium, Professional)

2. Sign-Up and Authentication

Users can register using their email address or sign in via Google or Apple.

After login, users must verify their email through a confirmation link.

Users must confirm that they are 18 years or older and accept the Privacy Policy before accessing the app.

3. User Experience

Users can chat with AI bots, add them to favorites, and follow other users.

Each user has a personal account with privacy options.

Users can create, edit, and delete their own AI characters.

Characters can be marked as private or public. Users are fully responsible for the content of their created characters.

Users can generate images and upload a custom profile avatar.

All data and messages are securely stored via Firebase infrastructure.

4. Account Management

Users can change their password, delete their messages, and permanently delete their accounts at any time.

5. Subscription Plans and Payment

The app offers three subscription tiers: Free, Premium, and Professional.

All payments are securely processed through Apple.

6. Data Security

All user data is stored using Firebase, which applies industry-standard security practices.

While we take all necessary precautions, no system can be completely secure. In the event of a data breach, we will take all appropriate steps to minimize impact.

7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content of their AI characters and interactions within the app.

Any content that violates legal or ethical standards is strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or removal.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.
